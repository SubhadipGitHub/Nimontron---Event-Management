Nimontron

নিমন্ত্রণ — invitations, RSVPs & plates, in one place.

A handsome little system for the guest list, the food counter, and the family's live dashboard — all sharing one event, each on its own page.

◆

Who are you?

Pick the page made for your role — each one only asks for what it needs.

🎫

I'm a guest

Find your invite, RSVP, and get your plate pass. Just your name and the event code.
🍽

I'm serving plates

Scan or type a guest's code at the food counter. Needs the event code + counter access code.
📋

I'm the organizer

Manage the guest list, invites, and settings — or start a brand new event. Needs the organizer access code.
📊

I'm the client

A live, read-only view of RSVPs and serving progress. Needs the client access code.

Planning something new?

Start a new event →

Access codes here are a light gate to keep casual visitors out — not bank-grade security. Don't use this for anything highly sensitive.
Each role above lives on its own page with its own code, so nobody needs more access than their job requires.
